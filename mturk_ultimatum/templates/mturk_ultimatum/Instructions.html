Instructions

First, every player will be randomly paired with another player. In other words, you will have a counterpart, but you will not be told who it is. Your identity will also remain hidden from your counterpart.

The two participants in a pair will have two different roles: the proposer and the responder. You will be assigned randomly to a role, and it will be displayed on the next page. The role assignment is persistent across all rounds.

The two of you will together receive {{ Constants.endowment }} in each round. The experiment is about how to divide this amount. In each round, the proposer will make the responder a take-it-or-leave-it offer, which the responder can accept or reject. If the offer is rejected, both will receive {{ Constants.payoff_if_rejected }}. The game will be played for {{ Constants.num_rounds }} rounds.

Results

{% if not group.use_strategy_method %} In the end, when both players have made their decision, a results page will be displayed.   • If the offer is rejected, both players will receive {{ Constants.payoff_if_rejected }}
